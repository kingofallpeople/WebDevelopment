SCRAMBLED EGGS

Eggs are one of the my favourate food.here is a recipe for deliciously rich protine

Ingrediants

  • 2 EGGS
  • 1 TBS BUTTER
  • 2 TBS CREAM

METHOD

  1. Melt the butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs

DISCLAIMER - Kids do not try this on your own. Please do it under your parents observation.
